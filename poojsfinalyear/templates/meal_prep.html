🍳 Smart Meal Prep Guide

Weekly Prep Strategy

Sunday (Prep Day)

  • Cook large batches of dal and rice
  • Prepare vegetable base curries
  • Chop and store vegetables
  • Prepare spice mixes and marinades

Wednesday (Mid-week Refresh)

  • Refresh green vegetables
  • Prepare quick breakfast items
  • Marinate proteins for next 3 days

Indian Cuisine Prep Tips

🌾 Grains & Lentils

  • Cook large batch of rice - lasts 3-4 days
  • Prepare different dals in pressure cooker
  • Pre-soak lentils overnight for faster cooking

🥬 Vegetables

  • Wash and chop vegetables on prep day
  • Store green vegetables separately
  • Blanch vegetables for longer storage

🌶️ Spices & Masalas

  • Prepare garam masala in bulk
  • Make ginger-garlic paste weekly
  • Store whole spices in airtight containers

🍞 Bread & Rotis

  • Make dough and store in fridge
  • Pre-make parathas and freeze
  • Keep store-bought backup options

Storage Solutions

🥘 Cooked Curries

Store in airtight containers. Refrigerate for 3-4 days, freeze for up to 1 month.

🍚 Rice & Grains

Cool completely before storing. Keep in fridge for 4-5 days.

🥗 Fresh Vegetables

Store in perforated bags in fridge. Use within 3-4 days for best quality.

Time-Saving Hacks

⚡ 15-Minute Meals

Keep pre-cooked dal, rice, and basic vegetables ready for quick assembly.

🥄 One-Pot Wonders

Khichdi, biryani, and pulao can be made in single pot with minimal cleanup.

❄️ Freezer Friends

Parathas, bread, and curry bases freeze well and reheat quickly.
