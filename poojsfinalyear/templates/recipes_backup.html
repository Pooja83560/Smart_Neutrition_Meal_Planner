👨‍🍳 Pro Cooking Tips

🧂 Spice Secrets

Always toast whole spices before grinding for maximum flavor. Store ground spices in airtight containers.

🍅 Perfect Base

Cook onions until golden brown for the best curry base. Don't rush this step - it's the foundation of flavor.

🥘 Rice Tips

Rinse rice until water runs clear. Use 1:1.5 ratio for rice to water for perfectly cooked grains.

⏲️ Timing

Start with ingredients that take longest to cook. Add delicate vegetables and herbs at the end.
